About FinanceTracker
Who we are and what we stand for
Our Mission
Why we built FinanceTracker

At FinanceTracker, we believe everyone deserves a clear picture of their financial health. Our mission is to make personal finance simple, transparent and accessible — giving you the tools to track your spending, manage your budgets and grow your wealth with confidence.

Built as a Cambrian College Capstone Project, FinanceTracker combines clean architecture, modern design and real-world financial features to help individuals take control of their money.

What We Offer
Expense Tracking
Log and categorise every transaction with ease
Budget Management
Set monthly limits and monitor utilization in real time
Income Tracking
Monitor salaries, freelance income and investments

Net Worth Dashboard

Privacy & Data Safety
Encrypted Passwords
All passwords are hashed with BCrypt — never stored in plain text
JWT Authentication
Every session uses a signed token that expires automatically
Secure Password Reset
Reset links are single-use and expire after 30 minutes
Your Data is Yours
We never sell or share your financial data with third parties
Local by Default
Your database runs on your own machine, not shared cloud servers
No Ads, Ever
FinanceTracker is completely ad-free — your attention is not our product
